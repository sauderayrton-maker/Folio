Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Two tools built to work the way you actually work — fast, private, and right in your browser.

Live preview

Watch your résumé update as you type — what you see is exactly what prints.

One-page fit meter

A live gauge tells you when your content fits, with a density slider to tighten it up.

Templates & colors

Ten distinct templates, seven accent colors, and a serif / sans toggle.

Cover letter

Write a matching cover letter that exports right alongside your résumé.

Autosave & backups

Saves to your browser as you work. Export your data to a file anytime.

Totally private

No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your data never leaves your device.

Cash flow breakdown

See your net income, taxes, expenses, and savings across monthly, biweekly, and yearly views — instantly.

Goal timelines

Set a target amount for any savings goal — Flow tells you exactly how many months until you get there.

Overtime + tax calc

Enter wage, hours, and tax rate — Flow handles the math including 1.5× overtime and gives you your real take-home.
